Sports

I competed as a student-athlete at the University of Michigan on the men's water polo team. We won four B1G Conference Championships, three of them consecutively from 2016 to 2018. I was named to the Collegiate Water Polo Association's Men's Scholar Athlete Team in recognition of combined academic and athletic achievement.
